Portfolio

Here, you'll discover a curated collection of my work, showcasing my research work, publications, musical writng, and the variety of my performances.

  • Artistic Research - Malen von Klängen

    I explored the intersection of art and science by investigating the interplay between colors, musical sounds, and emotions, employing both qualitative and quantitative methodologies to collect and analyze data, thereby uniting artistic research with empirical analysis to test hypotheses in the realm of art.

  • Publishing - Der Klang des Bild

    The relationship between visual art and music, as described in this artistic-musical book, is exemplified through the merging of representations and sound, featuring twelve selected images from various media, each paired with a quote and a musical score, all revolving around the leitmotif of the unpleasant and strong emotion of fear.

  • Artistic Project - Musikalische Verkehrszeichen

    Shapes, colours, words, symbols, and artistic elements such as musical notes and staves are used in traffic signs to convey messages, attract drivers' attention, and aid in organized, predictable driving, with musical notations and signs paralleling the visual representation systems of language, thereby potentially enhancing careful driving and fostering a sense of community among motorists and residents.
